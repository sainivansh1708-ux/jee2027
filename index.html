🔗 Google Sheets sync
Every time you save a day, your data is automatically sent to a Google Sheet in your Google Drive. Set it up once — works forever. Follow the steps below.
1
Go to sheets.google.com and create a new blank spreadsheet. Name it JEE 2027 Tracker.
2
In the spreadsheet, click Extensions → Apps Script. Delete everything in the editor.
3
Paste the script below into the editor and click Save (💾).
4
Click Deploy → New deployment. Type: Web app. Execute as: Me. Access: Anyone. Click Deploy and copy the URL.
5
Paste that URL in the box below and click Connect.

📱 Install as app
